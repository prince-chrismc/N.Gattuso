About

N.Gattuso is a professional DJ for private events and club events. Nicholas grew up in the mobile DJ industry learning everything about the field from his father, owner and CEO of Stardust Productions; 35 years of knowledge and experience has been passed down from father to son. At the age of 15, Nicholas officially began DJing at events. Seven years later, N.Gattuso is familiar with music of all styles and ages and is prepared to play for any crowd. With the keen ability of reading the crowd, reaching customer satisfaction and, many years of experience, N.Gattuso is the DJ needed for your event!
